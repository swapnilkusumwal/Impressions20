The Biotechnology Hub of JIIT provides a platform for enhancing skills of budding scientists and technicians alike by integrating and considering all spheres of engineering. The hub believes in the principle of "unity in diversity" but summed up as a different concept, "Integration is unity personified!". From the technical events like Hydrophilic to analytical ones like Sherlocked, the hub aims for an overall development of the students by not forgetting the real essence of biotechnology.
