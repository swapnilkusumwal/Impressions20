IOE

It’s Our Earth aims at carrying out activities that help in welfare of the society and help in improving the environment. The activities range from spreading awareness to carrying out donation drives. The main aim is carrying out activities so that we can help the needy, push the social barriers and bring about the change that is needed in today’s society.
