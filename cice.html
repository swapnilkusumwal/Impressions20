CICE

Creativity and innovation cell in electronics is the technical hub of the core electronics department. Not only we teach the student about several integrated circuits and some useful components of electronics but also we redound them to develop some eminence idea to outbid their knowledge toward forging some excellent project in the future. CICE was formed with a vision to promote the creative endeavours of electronics and encourage students to build projects that solve real-world problems. As a part of this endeavour, for many years, CICE has been conducting several workshops and events focusing on different domains of research in electronics engineering.
