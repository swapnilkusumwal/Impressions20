Jhankaar

The aims to instill a great love of dance, while inspiring self-confidence, discipline and respect of the performing arts. We recognize that every student has something to offer the arts and the arts has something for every student. We also conduct training sessions in our college like small workshops. Besides that, we even participate for competitions in many college fests and even perform in conferences, ethnic night and many other events that are held in our college. We have 3 dedicated troupes for the various dance forms namely, Music Made Visible (MMV)- The Western dance troupe, Nrityang- The Indian Classical dance troupe and Surkhaab- The Bhangra troupe.
