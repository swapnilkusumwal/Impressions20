JPEG

The Jaypee Photo Enthusiasts guild aims at promoting the extra-curricular endeavours in the field of photography and cinematography amidst the technical hubs in the college. From organising workshops on photography and post-processing to organising excursions to historical sites around Delhi, we not only aim at imparting knowledge in our domain, but insights into our culture as well. The core team at JPEG is a composition of 8 members and our faculty advisor Mr. Manish Kumar Thakur, who passionately work together in great cohesion for achieving the grail that has been sought since the hubs very inception.
