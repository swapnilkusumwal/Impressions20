GDH

The official Game Development hub of JIIT aims to enhance the skills needed to create professional-looking games, visual effects, and help students in developing their gaming skills and developing and building games. Game development hub was formed with a vision to promote game development in our college and provide opportunities to the students in the field. From last several years we have been conducting several workshops and competitive events on different platforms of game development.
