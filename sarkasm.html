Sarkasm

SARKASM aims to create a platform for young stand-up comedians and help them improve their comedy skills. Sarkasm also helps individuals to start their journey as full-time stand-up comedian by giving them opportunities to perform outside college too. The hub conducts Open Mic event and competition like stand up competition and meme competition. We also invite proclaimed stand up comedians to perform in our college. Sarkasm gives you a platform where you can showcase your comic talent and by doing this you become more confident, your public speaking skills increase, public engagement skill increases and you become fearless.
